1. Sister in law : 
    1.1 husband's elder sister: বড়ো ননদ (bodo nanad)
    1.2 husband's younger sister : ছোট ননদ (choto nanad)
    1.3 elder brother's wife: বড়ো জা (bodo ja)
    1.4 younger brother's wife: ছোট জা (choto ja)
    1.5 wife's elder sister: বড়ো শালী (bodo shaali)
    1.6 wife's younger sister: ছোট শালী (choto shaali)
    
2. Brother in law:
    2.1 wife's elder brother: বড়ো শালা (bodo shala)
    2.2 wife's younger brother: ছোট শালা (choto shala)
    2.3 sister's husband: জামাই বাবু (jamai babu)
    2.5 husband's elder brother:  ভাসুর (bhasur)
    2.6 husband's younger brother: দেওর (daor)
   
3. Uncle :
    3.1 father's elder brother: জেঠা মশাই (jetha mosai)
    3.2 father's younger brother: কাকু (kaku)
    3.3 mother's brother: মামা (mama)
    3.5 father's sister's husband: পিশে মশাই (pishe moshai)
    3.7 mother's sister's husband: মেশো মশাই (mesho moshai)
    
4. Aunt:
    4.1 father's sister: পিসি মা (pishi ma) 
    4.3 mother's sister: মাসী মা (mashi ma)
    4.5 father's elder brother's wife: জেঠি মা (jethi ma)
    4.6 father's younger brother's wife: কাকি মা (kaki ma)
    4.7 mother's brother's wife: মামী মা (mami ma)
    
5. Grandfather : ঠাকুরদা (dadu) ; Nanaji is দাদু

6. Grandmother:
    6.1 father's mother: ঠাকু মা (thaku ma)
    6.2 mother's mother: দিদি মা (didi ma)

9. Cousin:
    9.0 Does this depend on age and say simply brother and sister or call by names?
    9.1 father's elder brother's son: জেঠতুতো দাদা/ভাই (দাদা when the cousin is elder, ভাই when cousin is younger) 
    9.2 father's younger brother's son:  খুড়তুতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.3 mother's elder brother's son: মামাতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.4 mother's younger brother' son: মামাতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.5 father's elder sister's son: পিসতুতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.6 father's younger sister's son: পিসতুতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.7 mother's elder sister's son: মাসতুতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.8 mother's younger sister's son: মাসতুতো দাদা/ভাই
    9.9 father's elder brother's daughter: জেঠতুতো দিদি/বোন (দিদি when  elder sister, বোনষষ when younger sister) 
    9.10 father's younger brother's daughter:  খুড়তুতো দিদি/বোন
    9.11 mother's elder brother's daughter: মামাতো দিদি/বোন
    9.12 mother's younger brother' daughter: মামাতো দিদি/বোন
    9.13 father's elder sister's daughter: পিসতুতো দিদি/বোন
    9.14 father's younger sister's daughter: পিসতুতো দিদি/বোন
    9.15 mother's elder sister's daughter: মাসতুতো দিদি/বোন
    9.16 mother's younger sister's daughter: মাসতুতো দিদি/বোন
